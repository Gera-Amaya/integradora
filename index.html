Renewable energies

By: Admin 234 123

The renewable energy career focuses on developing sustainable solutions for energy generation, reducing dependence on fossil fuels.

Students explore various areas, such as solar, wind, hydroelectric, geothermal and biomass energy, as well as energy efficiency. Technologies include solar panels, wind turbines and the use of natural sources such as water and terrestrial heat.

Professionals in this field work to address challenges such as generation intermittency and play a crucial role in the transition to a more sustainable future, with job opportunities in energy companies, environmental consultancies and technological development.

Mechatronics Engineering

By: Admin 456 264

Mechatronics engineering combines mechanical, electronic and control disciplines to design and create intelligent and automated systems. Students immerse themselves in the study of mechanics, electronics, computing, and automation, acquiring skills to develop and maintain complex systems that integrate hardware and software.

The applications are varied, ranging from robotics to industrial automation and artificial intelligence.

Mechatronics are versatile professionals who find opportunities in sectors such as manufacturing, automotive, healthcare and technology. The interdisciplinarity of mechatronics makes it crucial in the era of automation and digitalization.

Graphic design

By: Admin 431 312

The graphic design career focuses on visual creation and aesthetic communication through various media. Students learn to combine art, technology and messaging to develop impactful and effective images.

They explore design principles, typography, color, and specialized software to create visual elements such as logos, posters, websites, and more. In addition, creativity and personal expression are encouraged.

Graphic designers work in creative agencies, marketing departments, publishing houses, and can also opt for freelance projects. In an increasingly visual world, graphic design plays a crucial role in building brands and transmitting persuasive and attractive messages.
